# TalkBridge Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

TalkBridge is a communication aid that converts speech to text and text to speech to help deaf and hard-of-hearing people hold conversations.

## Data Collection

TalkBridge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- Microphone audio is processed for speech recognition only. Audio is not recorded, saved, or sent to us. On supported devices, speech recognition runs entirely on your device; on some devices, Android's speech recognition service (provided by Google) may process audio according to Google's privacy policy.
- Typed text and conversation transcripts exist only on your screen during use. They are cleared when you reset the conversation or close the app, and are never transmitted anywhere.
- Settings (such as text size) are stored locally on your device only.

TalkBridge contains no analytics, no advertising, no user accounts, and makes no network connections.

## Permissions

TalkBridge requests one permission: RECORD_AUDIO (microphone), used solely for live speech-to-text during conversations.

## Children's Privacy

TalkBridge does not collect data from anyone, including children.
